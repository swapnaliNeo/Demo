Physics Immersion Program

The Immersion Program in Physics is conducted for college students from across the country who are pursuing physics at the Bachelors and Masters levels.

Our Goal

The Infosys Science Foundation believes in nurturing and encouraging students and budding scientists. Our programs that target students and educators expose them to the wider world of research and academia. Hands on workshops, interactions with towering intellects like Amartya Sen, Manjul Bhargava and others, ongoing programs like Limit Infinity and the Physics Immersion Program are all part of our endeavor to nurture the scholars and teachers of tomorrow.

Our Partners

The Infosys Science Foundation has partnered with the Physics Training and Talent Search (PTTS) to run the Immersion Program in Physics.

Learning From The Best

Along with hands on workshops and experiments, participating students get an opportunity to interact with physicists from around the world. Top scientists deliver exclusive talks and engage with the students as well.

Experts from among Infosys Prize laureates and jurors who have been part of the Immersion Program include Prof. Srinivas Kulkarni (CalTech), Prof. Rajaram Nityananda (Azim Premji University), Prof. Ashoke Sen (HRI, Allahabad), and Prof G. Ravindrakumar (TIFR).
