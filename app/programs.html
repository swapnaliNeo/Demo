The Infosys Science Foundation believes in nurturing and encouraging students and budding scientists. Our programs that target students and educators expose them to the wider world of research and academia. Hands on workshops, interactions with towering intellects like Amartya Sen, Manjul Bhargava and others, ongoing programs like Limit Infinity and the Physics Immersion Program are all part of our endeavor to nurture the scholars and teachers of tomorrow.

Educators Gnanadeepa

The Infosys Science Foundation's Teacher's Training Program, Gnanadeepa has become an annual program that targets teachers and teachers in training at the grassroots. We believe that good teachers are the key to encouraging curiosity, a questioning mind and a thirst for knowledge among students.

researchers Infosys Science Foundation - Springer Series

The Infosys Science Foundation collaborated with global publisher Springer to publish The Infosys Science Foundation Series of academic books. The series contains high quality books on topics like and other content written, edited, or vetted by Infosys Prize laureates and jury members on a range of current topics. They are aimed at a diverse audience of researchers, students, educators, and professionals.

Public Engaging the Public

Creating spaces for open dialogue engages people in thinking about complex and contentious issues in their communities. We can no longer use the so-called ‘Sagan Effect’ as an excuse and lock away research in the ivory towers of academia.
