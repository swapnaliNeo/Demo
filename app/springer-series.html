Infosys Science Foundation – Springer Series

The Infosys Science Foundation collaborated with global publisher Springer to publish The Infosys Science Foundation Series of academic books. The series contains high quality books on topics like and other content written, edited, or vetted by Infosys Prize laureates and jury members on a range of current topics. They are aimed at a diverse audience of researchers, students, educators, and professionals.

Infosys Science Foundation Series In Applied Sciences And Engineering

This sub-series focuses on high-quality content in applied sciences and the various disciplines of engineering, including materials science and computer science.
